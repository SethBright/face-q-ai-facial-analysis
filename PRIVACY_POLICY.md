# Privacy Policy

**Looks IQ | AI Facial Analysis**

Last updated: February 2025

---

## 1. Introduction

We are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our Looks IQ mobile application and related services (“the App”).

---

## 2. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of information:

- **Photos and image data:** Front and side selfie images you upload for facial analysis. These are used only to generate your analysis and are not used to identify you personally.
- **Analysis results:** AI-generated scores and recommendations (e.g. overall rating, skin quality, jawline, grooming tips) that we store locally on your device so you can view your scan history.
- **Usage data:** Information about how you use the App (e.g. features used, scan count) to improve the service and your experience.
- **Device information:** Technical data such as device type and operating system, where necessary for the App to function and for support.
- **Purchase and subscription data:** If you subscribe or make in-app purchases, we and our payment/subscription provider may process transaction data to fulfill and manage your subscription.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide AI facial analysis and personalised grooming and skincare recommendations.
- Store your scan history and results on your device so you can access them in the App.
- Operate subscriptions and in-app purchases.
- Improve the App, fix issues, and develop new features.
- Respond to your support requests when you contact us.
- Comply with legal and regulatory requirements where applicable.

We do not sell your personal information or your photos to third parties.

---

## 4. How Your Photos Are Processed

- When you submit photos for analysis, image data is sent to our AI provider (Google Gemini) to generate your results. This processing is necessary to deliver the analysis feature.
- We do not use your photos for advertising, marketing, or training AI models for purposes unrelated to your analysis. We recommend reviewing the AI provider’s privacy policy for their handling of data sent via their API.
- Scan thumbnails and results may be stored locally on your device (e.g. in local storage) so you can view past scans. You can delete scans and clear data from within the App or your device settings where supported.

---

## 5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

- **Service providers:** With trusted providers who help us run the App (e.g. AI analysis, subscription/payment processing), under strict confidentiality and data protection obligations.
- **Legal:** When required by law, court order, or to protect our or others’ legal rights.
- **With your consent:** Where you have given explicit consent for a specific use or disclosure.

---

## 6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your information, including:

- Use of secure connections and practices where data is transmitted or stored.
- Limiting access to personal and image data on a need-to-know basis.
- Reliance on reputable third-party providers that follow industry security standards.

Data stored only on your device is subject to your device’s security (e.g. passcode, biometrics). You are responsible for keeping your device secure.

---

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- **Access** the personal information we hold about you.
- **Correct** inaccurate or incomplete information.
- **Request deletion** of your personal information, where applicable.
- **Object or restrict** certain processing of your information.
- **Data portability** where technically feasible.
- **Withdraw consent** where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights or ask questions about our data practices, contact us at **bestillhelp@gmail.com**. You may also use in-app options to delete scans and clear locally stored data where available.

---

## 8. Data Retention

- **On your device:** Scan history and related data are kept until you delete them or clear the App’s data.
- **With us and our providers:** We retain information only as long as needed to provide the service, handle support, and meet legal obligations. Where we do not need data anymore, we delete or anonymise it.

---

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own (e.g. where our AI or cloud providers operate). We ensure that such transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards in line with applicable data protection laws.

---

## 10. Children

The App is not intended for users under the age of 13 (or higher where local law requires). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us at **bestillhelp@gmail.com** and we will take steps to delete it.

---

## 11. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy (e.g. in the App or on our website) and updating the “Last updated” date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy where permitted by law.

---
